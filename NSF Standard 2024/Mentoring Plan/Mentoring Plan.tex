\title{Mentoring Plan}

\maketitle

\noindent \textbf{Orientation:} The PI will meet with all students and post-docs (none planned) associated with this grant to have a careful conversation surrounding the goals of this proposal, their career and educational goals, and the expectations for participating in these projections. These expectations and commitments from both the student/post-doc and the PI will be recorded in writing and frequently used as a benchmark to judge progress and make adjustments.   
\\
\\
\textbf{Professional Advising:} In addition to the research advising the PI will provide students/post-docs with mentorship and advice on how they can move towards achieving their professional goals. This will include both informal advising and formal advising at scheduled monthly meetings specifically designated towards conversations around professional goals. 
\\
\\
\textbf{Preparing Publications:} The PI will provide the students/postdocs with advice and suggestions for how to approach academic publishing, both in theory and in practice with any publications that may result from this work. 
\\
\\
\textbf{Networking:} The PI will help students and post-docs identify and apply to attend conferences and workshops appropriate for building their professional and research networks.
